Root mid-July update

Hello there!

This letter is the first part of many such updates that we’ll be sending out in the future to let you folks know how things are going on at Root. Our early access program went live (finally!) just last week and it’s with great excitement that we’re writing to you.

Root at its core is aimed at helping creators monetize their work & help them build lasting communities by leveraging the power of web3 and NFTs. This project has been a year in the making, fueled by the desire of helping creators thrive on the internet. And so, here we are chipping away at that goal week-on-week by learning & building for these communities across the globe.

So without further ado, let’s get straight to it!

Our progress so far

We’re testing waters with an MVP solving a very basic need for creators right now - a holistic storefront to showcase all of their work across web2 & web3. Prima facie, this has garnered a lot of interest from the early set of creators we’ve talked to.

Right now we’re actively onboarding creators to gather feedback on the MVP and also to get them to transition from the use of “Link-in-bio” products to using their profiles on Root to showcase their NFTs instead (and in the future directly sell from there).

We’ve released Root Community NFTs for our early adopters!

This NFT collection will serve as a Proof-of-Concept on how creators can leverage our minting & utility stack to engage with their community members on Root.

Our early access program creators and collectors will receive:

  • NFT-gated access on our discord and on Root to share feedback
  • Regular product updates from the Root team
  • Lifetime premium access
  • Vote on new features

Some of the features we have off the bat

In addition to aggregating NFTs across blockchains & wallets and setting up your web3 storefront, creators also get a chance to carry all their NFTs in one single place with our Android & iOS app. They can even “cast” their NFTs to any Android TV to showcase their work when they head over to a friend’s party or a web3 meetup.

We currently support Ethereum, Polygon, Binance Smart Chain & Avalanche blockchains, and we’re actively working towards adding support for Tezos & Solana as well.

What are we currently developing?

We’re currently focusing on 3 areas which are core to our core product offering:-

  • Minting with benefits - Creators can easily mint NFTs without paying gas fees and sell these community-focused NFTs on their Root storefronts.
  • Simplified payment & integrated wallet - Collectors will be able to easily purchase by using just their email address & using their debit cards.
  • NFT gated features on the storefront - “unlockable links”, “exclusive creator posts”, “access to private discord server” and more!

For piloting this product we’re tying up with artists & creative agencies with access to a good set of creators who’re interested in the space.

These features are expected to be live by the end of September ‘22.

What is our target for the early access program

We intend to onboard 20 creators by the end of July.

And we’ve set a target of achieving at least 200 creators by the end of 2022, and this is assuming we get our minting + payment stack ready by the end of September ‘22

What we need help with

  • Since we’re releasing our first custom smart contract for creators, we’re looking to get our smart contracts audited. We would love to get introductions to blockchain advisors, especially those who could lend us advice on how to go about auditing & ensuring the security of our smart contracts.
  • Company registration also has been a tricky subject and we’ve been actively exploring options in Singapore + British Virgin Islands, UAE (specifically Abu Dhabi Global Market), and London. If you have any advice on the matter or know someone who can help us with it, please do introduce us to them.
  • In addition to that, getting access to creators or creator-first businesses & agencies that you know would also be of great help.

Apologies for the long update, but we felt all of you would’ve loved to have a refresher regardless. Looking forward to hearing for you.

Best regards,

Nitin Shaji

Co-founder & CEO, Root
